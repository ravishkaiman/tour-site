6 Nights / 7 Days Sri Lanka Tour

Embark on an unforgettable 7-day journey exploring Sri Lanka’s most iconic destinations and hidden gems. From the bustling streets of Colombo to the ancient wonders of Dambulla and Sigiriya, the cultural charm of Kandy, the breathtaking hill country tea plantations, and the thrilling wildlife encounters at Yala National Park, this tour blends culture, nature, and adventure into one seamless experience. Discover the beauty of Ella’s misty landscapes, ride the world-famous scenic train, and unwind along the golden beaches of Sri Lanka’s southern coast. With handpicked accommodations and carefully curated activities, this itinerary promises memories that will last a lifetime.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore Ancient Heritage – Visit the awe-inspiring Dambulla Cave Temples and the world-famous Sigiriya Rock Fortress, both UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
  • Wildlife Adventures – Experience thrilling safaris at Minneriya and Yala National Parks, spotting elephants, leopards, and other exotic wildlife.
  • Cultural Charm of Kandy – Discover the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic and immerse yourself in the city’s rich traditions.
  • Scenic Hill Country Experience – Enjoy a tea plantation visit and ride the world-renowned train through breathtaking landscapes.
  • Breathtaking Ella – Hike to Little Adam’s Peak, marvel at the Nine Arches Bridge, and feel the rush of the Flying Ravana Zip Line.
  • Relax on the Southern Coast – Unwind along Sri Lanka’s golden beaches and soak in the coastal beauty.

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 – Arrival at Colombo

  • Pickup at Colombo Airport, transfer to Colombo (40 mins).
  • Check-in at hotel.
  • Evening Colombo city highlights: Gangaramaya Temple, Lotus Tower, Red Mosque, Independence Arcade, shopping & dining.
  • Overnight stay in Colombo.

Day 02 – Dambulla Cave Temples & Minneriya Safari

  • Breakfast at hotel, head to Dambulla/Sigiriya.
  • Visit Dambulla Cave Temple en route.
  • Traditional Sri Lankan village lunch with activities.
  • Check-in at Sigiriya hotel.
  • Afternoon safari at Minneriya National Park (2–6 pm).
  • Overnight stay in Sigiriya.

Day 03 – Sigiriya Rock Fortress & Kandy

  • Morning climb at Sigiriya Rock Fortress.
  • Breakfast at hotel.
  • Travel to Kandy with stops at Spice Garden and Matale Hindu Temple.
  • Evening Kandy city tour: Cultural Dance Show, Temple of the Tooth, Kandy View Point, Bahirawakanda Big Buddha.
  • Overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 04 – Hill Country & Scenic Train Ride

  • Breakfast at hotel, travel towards Nuwara Eliya.
  • Tea estate & factory visit.
  • Stops at Ramboda Falls, Ramboda Viewpoint, Hanuman Temple.
  • Explore Nuwara Eliya city: Gregory Lake, Victoria Park, old Post Office.
  • Catch 2:30 pm train to Ella (reserved seats). Chauffeur meets at Ella station.
  • Overnight stay in Ella.

Day 05 – Ella & Yala National Park

  • Breakfast at hotel.
  • Morning Ella tour: Little Adam’s Peak, Nine Arches Bridge, Flying Ravana Zip Line, Ravana Falls.
  • Travel to Yala National Park (depart 11:30 am).
  • Afternoon safari (2–6 pm).
  • Overnight stay in Yala.

Day 06 – Southern Sri Lanka

  • Breakfast at hotel, travel towards Galle/Mirissa/Bentota.
  • Stops: Weligama stilt fishermen, Coconut Tree Hill Mirissa, Unawatuna Beach.
  • Tour Old Dutch Fort in Galle.
  • Visit Turtle Hatchery & Madu River Safari.
  • Overnight stay at Galle/Mirissa/Bentota.

Day 07 – Departure

  • Breakfast at hotel.
  • Leisure time depending on flight schedule.
  • Transfer to Colombo Airport for departure.

Sri Lanka Honeymoon Itinerary – 3 Nights / 4 Days

Embark on an unforgettable 3 Nights/4 Days journey through the heart of Sri Lanka, exploring the island’s most iconic destinations. Begin your adventure with the awe-inspiring Sigiriya Rock Fortress, the sacred Dambulla Cave Temple, and an exciting safari tour. Continue into the misty hill country, where you’ll enjoy a scenic train ride and a traditional tea experience. Discover the natural beauty and charm of Ella, then head south to relax on the serene beaches of Bentota. Conclude your journey with a brief tour of vibrant Colombo. A perfect blend of culture, nature, and relaxation awaits!

Tour Highlights

  • Climb the Iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress – Discover the ancient marvel and UNESCO World Heritage Site with breathtaking panoramic views.
  • Explore the Sacred Dambulla Cave Temple – Visit one of Sri Lanka’s most impressive temple complexes filled with centuries-old murals and Buddha statues.
  • Experience a Scenic Train Ride through the Hill Country – Enjoy one of the world’s most beautiful train journeys, passing lush tea plantations and misty mountains.
  • Discover the Charm of Ella – Wander through the picturesque mountain village of Ella, with highlights like Nine Arches Bridge and Little Adam’s Peak.
  • Relax on the Golden Beaches of Bentota – Unwind on the tranquil southern coastline before wrapping up your journey with a glimpse of Colombo.
  • Wildlife Safari Adventure – Spot elephants, leopards, and exotic birds in their natural habitat.

Tour Itinerary

Day 01: Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Dambulla & Safari Tour. Visit Dambulla Cave Temple, climb Sigiriya, enjoy a traditional village safari with lunch, safari at Minneriya National Park, cultural show in Habarana, and overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 02: Kandy City Tour, Temple of the Tooth Relic, Kandy Lake, Tea Factory visit, Ramboda Falls, and the scenic train journey to Ella. Overnight stay in Ella.

Day 03: Explore Ella (Little Adam’s Peak, Flying Ravana Zip Line, Ravana Falls, Nine Arches Bridge, Ella Rock) and transfer to Bentota. Overnight stay in Bentota.

Day 04: Relax at Bentota Beach, enjoy water sports, Madu River Boat Safari, Turtle Hatcheries, Colombo City Tour (Gangaramaya Temple, Red Mosque, Lotus Tower, Independence Square, shopping malls). Transfer to Colombo Airport/Hotel.

About Ella, Sri Lanka

Ella is a small town located in the Uva Province, belonging to the Badulla District, around 225 km from Colombo. The Kandy to Ella train ride is world-renowned as one of the most scenic train journeys in the world. The best time to visit Ella is from January to March.

Guides will take you through Ella City while sharing valuable insights. English-speaking guides are standard, and other languages can be arranged upon request.

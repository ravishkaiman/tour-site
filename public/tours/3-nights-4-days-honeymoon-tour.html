3 Nights 4 Days Honeymoon Tour

Discover the enchanting beauty of Sri Lanka on this 3-night, 4-day romantic getaway. Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, and serene tranquility of this tropical paradise. From the bustling city of Colombo to the serene hill country of Nuwara Eliya, this itinerary offers a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and romance.

Tour Highlights

  • Climb the Iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress – UNESCO World Heritage Site with panoramic views.
  • Explore the Sacred Dambulla Cave Temple – centuries-old murals and Buddha statues.
  • Experience a Scenic Train Ride through the Hill Country.
  • Discover the Charm of Ella – Nine Arches Bridge & Little Adam’s Peak.
  • Relax on the Golden Beaches of Bentota.
  • Colombo City Exploration – vibrant streets & cultural landmarks.

Itinerary

Day 01 – Sigiriya, Dambulla & Safari

Pickup from airport or hotel → Visit Dambulla Cave Temple → Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress → Traditional village safari with lunch → Safari at Minneriya National Park → Cultural Show at Habarana → Transfer to Kandy with city viewpoint → Overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 02 – Kandy to Nuwara Eliya & Scenic Train

Kandy city tour including Temple of the Tooth, Kandy Lake & markets → Visit Tea Factory & Ramboda Falls → Gregory Lake Park → Scenic train journey from Nuwara Eliya to Ella → Overnight stay in Ella.

Day 03 – Explore Ella & Transfer to Bentota

Morning hike to Little Adam’s Peak (optional sunrise) → Flying Ravana Zipline → Ravana Falls → Nine Arches Bridge → Ella Rock → Transfer to Bentota → Evening beach relaxation → Overnight stay in Bentota.

Day 04 – Bentota & Colombo

Relax at Bentota Beach with optional water sports → Madu River Boat Safari → Turtle Hatcheries → Transfer to Colombo → Colombo city tour including Gangaramaya Temple, Red Mosque, Lotus Tower & Independence Square → Shopping at One Galle Face & Odel → Airport/Hotel drop-off.

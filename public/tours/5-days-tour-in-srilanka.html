Summer in Sri Lanka — 3 Day Short Tour

Quick, seasonal getaway covering culture, wildlife, hill country & marine life — ideal for a 3-day escape.
SigiriyaDambullaMinneriya SafariTrincomaleeKandyNuwara Eliya

Tour Overview

Experience the essence of Sri Lanka in just three unforgettable days. Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress, discover Dambulla Cave Temple, enjoy a Minneriya safari, go whale & dolphin watching in Trincomalee, take the scenic train to Nuwara Eliya, visit tea plantations and waterfalls, and finish in Colombo or Negombo.

Tour Highlights

Climb the Majestic Sigiriya Rock Fortress
Discover the Sacred Dambulla Cave Temple
Exciting Minneriya Safari
Hill country tea experience & train
Whale & dolphin watching at Trincomalee
Waterfalls: Devon & St. Clair's

Itinerary (3 Days)

Day 01
  • Colombo/Negombo → Dambulla Cave Temple → Sigiriya climb
  • Village lunch & Minneriya Safari
  • Overnight Trincomalee
Day 02
  • Whale & dolphin watching
  • Nilaveli, Trinco city sites
  • Spice Garden & Kandy Temple of Tooth
  • Overnight Kandy
Day 03
  • Train Kandy → Nuwara Eliya
  • City tour, Tea estate visit
  • Stops at Devon & St. Clair's Falls
  • Return to Colombo/Negombo

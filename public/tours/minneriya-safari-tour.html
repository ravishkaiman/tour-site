About Minneriya National Park

Located in Sri Lanka’s North Central Province, Minneriya is world-renowned for the largest gathering of Asian elephants, with over 300 elephants seen between July and September. The park surrounds the historic Minneriya Reservoir, built by King Mahasena in the 3rd Century AD. It is also home to over 160 bird species, sloth bears, leopards, and many other endemic animals.

A safari here is not just about wildlife—it’s about experiencing the harmony of elephants, watching mothers care for their calves, and enjoying the cooling breezes of the forest-covered park.

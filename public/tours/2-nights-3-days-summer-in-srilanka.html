Summer in Sri Lanka — 3 Day Short Tour

Quick, seasonal getaway covering culture, wildlife, hill country & marine life — ideal for a 3-day escape.

Tour Overview

Experience the essence of Sri Lanka in just three unforgettable days. Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress, discover Dambulla Cave Temple, enjoy a Minneriya safari, go whale & dolphin watching in Trincomalee, take the scenic train to Nuwara Eliya, visit tea plantations and waterfalls, and finish in Colombo or Negombo.

Itinerary (3 Days)

Day 01 — Sigiriya, Dambulla & Minneriya
  • Breakfast at hotel in Colombo/Negombo. Depart ~06:30 (approx. 3 hrs to Sigiriya).
  • En-route visit: Dambulla Cave Temple.
  • Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress (approx. 1.5–2 hrs).
  • Traditional village lunch, then Minneriya Safari (14:00–17:00).
  • Travel to Trincomalee for overnight stay.
Day 02 — Whale & Dolphin Watching, Travel to Kandy
  • Early morning whale watching (06:00–09:30). Visit Nilaveli beach and Trincomalee highlights.
  • Depart to Kandy around 11:00. Stop at spice gardens in Matale and Matale Hindu Temple.
  • Evening: Sri Lankan cultural show and Temple of the Tooth visit.
  • Overnight in Kandy.
Day 03 — Scenic Train, Tea Experience & Waterfalls
  • Breakfast and transfer to Kandy station for reserved-seat train to Nuwara Eliya (options: 08:53 or 11:03). Journey ~3.5 hrs.
  • Driver picks you at Nuwara Eliya station. Explore Gregory Lake, Victoria Park, Old Post Office.
  • Visit Pedro Tea Estate & factory. Depart for Colombo/Negombo with stops at Devon & St. Clair's Falls. Arrive late evening.

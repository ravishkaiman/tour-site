Why Visit Polonnaruwa?

Polonnaruwa offers a compact, walkable set of ruins that convey the scale and sophistication of medieval Sri Lanka. With clear inscriptions, superb stone sculpture, and a landscape shaped by ancient irrigation and agriculture, the site is both visually stunning and historically illuminating.
